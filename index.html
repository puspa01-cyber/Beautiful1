Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about APEX and Helix Robotics.

APEX is the second-generation humanoid robot from Helix Robotics — a 5'8", 62 kg general-purpose robot with 78 degrees of freedom, a 25 kg payload capacity, and a 10-hour operational runtime. It is designed to operate in any human-built environment without facility modifications.

APEX is purpose-built for manufacturing, logistics and warehousing, retail operations, and environments involving repetitive, ergonomically hazardous, or physically demanding tasks. Current deployments span automotive assembly, fulfillment center operations, and overnight retail restocking.

Height: 5'8" (173 cm) · Weight: 62 kg · Total DOF: 78 (16 per hand) · Walking speed: 1.6 m/s · Payload: 25 kg · Runtime: 10 hours · Onboard compute: Nvidia Jetson Thor · Vision: 4× RGB-D cameras with 360° coverage · LiDAR, IMU, full-body force/torque sensors · IP54 rated.

Most customers achieve productive task execution within 7–14 days of delivery. The HELIX Neural Engine learns customer-specific tasks from human demonstrations — no custom programming required. Our deployment team handles facility integration end-to-end.

APEX is certified to ISO 10218-1 (industrial robot safety), ISO/TS 15066 (collaborative robot force limits), CE marked for European deployment, and UL listed for North American facilities. Every unit ships with a full safety integration package.

APEX is available on a purchase or Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS) subscription model starting at $4,200/month per unit. Enterprise fleet pricing is available for orders of 10+ units. Most customers achieve full cost recovery within 24 months.

The HELIX Neural Engine is a multimodal AI system combining computer vision, proprioceptive feedback, and large language model reasoning. Operators demonstrate a task once; APEX builds a generalizable policy within hours. The system improves continuously via federated learning — every robot learns from every deployment, globally.
